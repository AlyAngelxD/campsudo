ABOUT

Postel is a site for you to create blog posts and share it with the world! On Postel, you can blog about whatever you want, and you can gain followers or follow other people. You can also customize the font, colors, text arrangement and more! Postel is an interactive page for people who love writing and reading stories/blog posts.
